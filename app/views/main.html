Importance of the US Dollar: The US Dollar is the most commonly converted currency in the world and is regularly used as a benchmark in the Forex market. As the dominant global reserve currency, it is held by nearly every central bank in the world. Additionally, the Dollar is used as the standard currency in the commodity market and therefore has a direct impact on commodity prices.

Apple

For software dev, it all comes down to what you're developing. If you're doing iOS or Linux-targeted dev, you'll find OSX easier to work on for the most part. If you're doing Windows-targeted dev, go with Windows. There are plenty of gray areas here: Windows does just fine with PHP, nodejs, MySQL, etc, and you can do serverside .NET dev just fine on an OSX machine - but you'll find it easier from a configuration/provisioning standpoint to stick with a dev environment that more closely matches your deployment one.

Globe

A globe is a three-dimensional, spherical, scale model of Earth (terrestrial globe or geographical globe) or other celestial body such as a planet or moon. While models can be made of objects with arbitrary or irregular shapes, the term globe is used only for models of objects that are approximately spherical.
